Our History

Samtse College of Education was founded by Bhutan's Third Dharma King, His Majesty Jigme Dorji Wangchuck. Launched as a Teacher Training Institute on 29th May 1968, the institute commenced its educational journey with only 41 students and a few teacher educators. Since its establishment, the College has given the country's education system over 5000 graduates with primary teaching certificates, bachelors, diplomas, postgraduate diplomas, and Masters qualifications.

The rich and varied learning experiences we offer our students are inspired by our philosophical commitment to the wisdom of right View, right Contemplation, and right Action, which collectively can cause the arising of the right Result.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in teacher education and educational research, nurturing holistic educators who embody the values of Gross National Happiness and contribute to the development of a just and harmonious society.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality teacher education that meets national and international standards
  • To promote research and innovation in education
  • To foster the values of Gross National Happiness
  • To develop professionally competent and morally upright educators
  • To contribute to the preservation and promotion of Bhutan's rich cultural heritage

Core Values

  • Excellence in Teaching and Learning
  • Integrity and Professionalism
  • Respect for Cultural Diversity
  • Environmental Stewardship
  • Community Engagement
